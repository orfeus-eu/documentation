ORFEUS Community-Project Support Grants
=======================================

ORFEUS established the Community-Project Support Fund in 2024.
Grants may be awarded to support the organisation of community events and networking activities
instrumental to the establishment of initiatives/projects relevant for the ORFEUS and European observational
seismology communities.
